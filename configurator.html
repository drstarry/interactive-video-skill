Step 5
Visual style
Match your content
Themes styled to match your subject. The visuals reinforce what you're teaching.
Chalkboard
Math, physics, and theory — the classroom. Dark slate with chalk-white text and yellow emphasis.
Terminal
Code, DevOps, and CLI — the developer's screen. Amber on dark with monospace type.
Notebook
Writing, languages, and humanities — the printed page. Cream background with serif headings and margin notes.
Blueprint
Engineering, architecture, and systems — the drafting table. Navy with grid lines and technical annotations.
Set the mood
General-purpose themes that work for any topic. Pick based on how you want it to feel.
Clean Dark
Distraction-free — content speaks for itself. Pure grayscale, no accent colors.
Clean Light
Bright and functional — easy on the eyes in daylight. Grey with teal accent, no decoration.
Studio
Warm and approachable — casual, hands-on learning. Kraft tan with orange accents and ruled lines.
Focus
Maximum concentration — near-black, no distractions. Restrained red accent, tight spacing.
